About Us

Computer Institute in Jalandhar

Webdox is an ISO certified computer institute in Jalandhar established in 2018 Near HMV College, Jalandhar. Our aim is to equip students with the necessary IT skills by focusing more on Practical Concepts of the course. IT companies - irrespective of the location it is situated in - expects a candidate to have a solid practical skill who can complete the job on time with decent quality, because at the end of the day they want their deadlines to be met. Therefore, we focus more on Live Projects and thus in a way building a portfolio for students which can latter be beneficial to get a Good Job in IT Sector.

We have been following this approach from last 5 years and many of our students are working in IT sector all around the world.
